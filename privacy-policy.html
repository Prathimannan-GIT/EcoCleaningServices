Privacy Policy

How EcoPure Cleaning Services collects, uses, and protects your information.

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

EcoPure Cleaning Services ("EcoPure", "we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information when you use our website, contact us, or book services:

  • Contact Information: name, email address, phone number.
  • Service Details: property type, service type, preferred date/time, special instructions.
  • Location Information: service address and access notes you provide.
  • Website Usage Data: basic analytics such as pages visited and approximate device/browser information (where available).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to inquiries and provide customer support.
  • Process booking requests and schedule services.
  • Provide quotes, confirmations, and service updates.
  • Improve our website and service experience.
  • Maintain business records and comply with legal requirements.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only when necessary to operate our business, such as with trusted service providers (for example, communication tools) or when required by law.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Your Choices

You may request to update or delete your personal information by contacting us. If you opted in to marketing emails, you may unsubscribe at any time.
