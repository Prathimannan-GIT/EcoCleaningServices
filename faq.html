About EcoPure Cleaning Services

Booking & Pricing

How to book and understand our pricing

While eco-cleaning may have a slightly higher upfront cost, the value is exceptional. Our prices are competitive with high-quality traditional cleaning services, and you get the added benefits of improved health, environmental protection, and superior cleaning results. Many clients find that the long-term health benefits and environmental impact make eco-cleaning an excellent investment.

Booking is easy! You can book online through our website booking form, call us directly at (555) 123-4567, or email info@ecopurecleaning.com. We'll discuss your specific needs, provide a detailed quote, and schedule a convenient time for your eco-cleaning service. The entire process typically takes less than 10 minutes.

Frequency depends on your needs and lifestyle. For most homes, bi-weekly cleaning maintains excellent results. Weekly service is ideal for busy families or those with pets. Monthly deep cleans work well for smaller households or as maintenance between more frequent cleanings. We'll help you determine the best schedule based on your specific situation.

We understand that plans change. Cancellations made at least 24 hours in advance receive a full refund or can be rescheduled at no charge. Cancellations within 24 hours may incur a 50% fee. We'll always work with you to find the best solution when unexpected circumstances arise.

Yes! We offer discounts for weekly and bi-weekly regular service clients. Weekly service receives a 15% discount, and bi-weekly service receives a 10% discount. We also offer special promotions for first-time clients and seasonal discounts throughout the year. Ask about our loyalty program for additional benefits.
