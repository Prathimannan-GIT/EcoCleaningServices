Our Story

Eco Cleaning was born from a simple realization: traditional cleaning products were harming our homes and our planet. Our founder, Sarah Martinez, experienced this firsthand when her child developed allergies linked to harsh chemical cleaners.

What started as a mission to find safer alternatives for her family evolved into a comprehensive eco-cleaning service dedicated to protecting both people and the environment. Today, we're proud to be California's leading green cleaning company.

Every service we deliver reflects our core values: sustainability, health, and exceptional quality. We believe that clean shouldn't compromise your wellbeing or our planet's future.

Our Mission & Values

Environmental Stewardship

We're committed to reducing our environmental footprint through sustainable practices, biodegradable products, and eco-friendly cleaning methods that protect our planet for future generations.

Health & Safety First

Your family's health is our priority. We use only non-toxic, hypoallergenic products that create safe indoor environments without compromising cleaning effectiveness.

Excellence in Service

Community Focus
